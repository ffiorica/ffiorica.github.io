Francesco Fiorica is a clinical oncologist treating more than 600 patients per year with an integrated approach with radiotherapy and cancer drugs (immunotherapy, target therapy, and chemotherapy).

He is using the most modern technology of radiotherapy (IMRT. VMAT, IGRT, SBRT, Breath Hold technique) in clinical practice, he gives priority to hypofractionation with stereotactic and with systemic therapy. Actually, he is the Head of Radiotherapy and Nuclear Medicine in AULSS 9 Scaligera (Verona).

In clinical practice, daily he sees great differences in results among the patients treated with various all modalities of radiotherapy.
